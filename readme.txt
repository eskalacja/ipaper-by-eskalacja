Manage and embed scribd ipaper documents and display it easily in your posts

== Description ==

You can manage your existing documents in your scribd account. Insert your document in one click into your posts.

For support and further information about ipaper plugin see the plugins homepage at [iPaper homepage](http://www.telesphore.org/plugins/ipaper "Link to iPaper homepage").

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin
1. Upload the `ipaper` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` folder
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Define your scribd login datas into iPaper settings
1. Synchronize your documents
